Account Deletion Request

App Name: Eco Fetch

Developer: Phiko Tshetsha

How to request account deletion

To delete your Eco Fetch account and all associated data, please send an email to:

phiko.tshetsha@gmail.com

Include your registered email address or phone number and use the subject line: Account Deletion Request

After receiving your request, we will verify your identity and confirm once the deletion process has started.

You may also request deletion through the app (if available in Settings → Account → Delete Account).

Important notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Authentication and login data
  • App usage and activity data
  • Any user-generated content linked to Eco Fetch

Data retention

Your Eco Fetch account data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. Some data may be retained for up to 90 days for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes.
